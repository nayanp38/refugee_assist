Our Mission

Refugee Assist empowers refugees and displaced individuals by leveraging technology to streamline their resettlement journeys. Using an AI-powered chatbot available 24/7 in over 18 languages, Refugee Assist provides personalized, culturally sensitive assistance.

The platform addresses vital needs like legal services, housing, employment, cultural integration, and mental health support, ensuring refugees receive accurate information tailored to their unique circumstances. Through technology, Refugee Assist aims to enhance the lives of refugees worldwide by making the complex process of resettlement more accessible and manageable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Refugee Assist?

Refugee Assist is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting refugees during the critical phase of resettlement through artificial intelligence (AI). We have developed a chatbot that provides real-time assistance and information to help refugees navigate legal issues, housing, employment, cultural integration, and physical and mental health resources.

How does the Refugee Assist chatbot work?

The chatbot operates through a simple interface where refugees can type their queries or needs. Based on the information provided by the user, such as language preference, origin, and location, the chatbot delivers personalized responses and connects users with relevant resources and support services in their area.

What kind of assistance does Refugee Assist provide?

Refugee Assist can help educate on a range of subjects:

  • Local legal services.
  • Housing support and information on available accommodations.
  • Employment resources.
  • Cultural integration tips.
  • Mental health guidance.

Is the Refugee Assist chatbot available in multiple languages?

Yes, the chatbot supports 18+ different languages to ensure accessibility for refugees from diverse linguistic backgrounds. Currently, languages such as Arabic, Spanish, Ukrainian, and more are supported, with plans to expand language capabilities based on user needs and feedback.

What if I have specific questions or need personalized assistance not covered by the chatbot?

While the chatbot provides comprehensive support, each refugee's situation may be unique. If you have specific questions or need further assistance beyond what the chatbot offers, please reach out to local refugee agencies. Ask the chatbot to learn more about these agencies in your area.

How can I contribute to Refugee Assist's mission?

You can support Refugee Assist's mission in several ways:

  • Spread awareness about Refugee Assist among refugee communities and local organizations.
  • Volunteer your time or expertise to help enhance the chatbot's capabilities.
  • Partner with us to expand our reach and improve the quality of educational resources available to refugees worldwide.

How does Refugee Assist ensure cultural sensitivity and inclusivity?

Refugee Assist incorporates cultural understanding into its chatbot responses. We collaborate with cultural advisors and refugee communities to ensure that our information and assistance are respectful and mindful of diverse cultural backgrounds, minimizing miscommunication and establishing inclusivity.
